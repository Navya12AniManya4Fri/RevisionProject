Gansta Granny

Description-

Ben is 11, an only child and a poor reader, whose ambition is to be a plumber. Every Friday he visits his Granny, while his parents go to watch ballroom dancing, and he hates those dull, smelly, cabbage-dominated evenings.

Author-

David Walliams Comedian

Moral of the Story

The moral of the story is 'don't assume old people are boring just because they are old'. In fact, they are likely to have had a much more interesting life than yours. Talk to old folk, listen to their stories. They are bound to be full of magic and wonder.

Billionare Boy

Description-

The story follows Joe Spud, who is the richest boy in the country and has everything he could ever want, but wishes to have a friend and eventually learns a lesson on being a normal boy.

Author-

David Walliams Comedian

Moral of the Story

What is the moral of Billionaire Boy? It follows the moral, "money can't buy you love". At times you can really sympathise with Joe, but at others, you just think that he's a spoilt brat. Full of touching moments, and some really funny, but odd characters, Billionaire Boy, is sure to be an instant bestseller.

The World's Worst Children is a collection of 10 stories, featuring 10 awfully dreadful children. Each story introduces us to a wicked & mischievous kid. The collection is illustrated by Tony Ross.
